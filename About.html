About Us

Who we are

The glass bead used is gentle enough to clean any surface while also giving just about any material a smooth even satin finish but is gentle enough to not eat up any of the part that is being vapor blasted. Vapor blasting offers the highest quality results for renewing aluminum, brass, and plastics. This process is also ideal for converting chrome to a satin finish without replacing perfectly good parts to get the look you want. You can expect the same quality work and friendly service whether you have a handful of parts or a hundred. I’m really enjoying thinking outside the box about what can be cleaned up from rubber intake boots, old tool restoration, abs plastic chrome emblems, chrome plated steel, copper, aluminum, magnesium and the list goes on. Look around and let’s turn some trash into treasure!

We also offer conventional dry media blasting for carbon steel and other materials requiring a more aggressive approach. We normally use black and white aluminum oxide, Walnut shell sand , garnet, or crushed glass media. These produce excellent results with less risk to the substrate. As well as being more environmentally friendly, creates less dust, and allows us to keep costs down for the customer.

Walnut shell blasting is an excellent solution for carbon covered intakes, exhausts, pistons, and heads. Removes the toughest build up with no damage to engine components. It is also very effective for prepping painted, dirty, oily, and greasy parts for vapor blasting.
